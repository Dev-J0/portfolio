In 2020 my partner gifted me the "Web Coding and Development for Dummies" and I fell in love with coding shortly after. I came to realize a bit later that the book wasn't actually covering most of the concepts I needed to know as a developer; it was only covering some basic fundamentals.

So I headed to Codecademy and completed most of the courses. I used freeCodeCamp as well. In the end, I decided to join The Odin Project just to better my skills and be able to create actual projects.

Being a Developer certainly means one can never truly master the field, but that's the beauty of it. I am on a continuous learning journey.
